Outer Box · Matte Black Rigid

The packaging is the first argument. Before the scent, before the bottle, the box has to communicate that you just bought something that matters.

The reference is Toledo steel — the armor worn by the Spanish knights of the Reconquista. Cold, heavy, precise. Gunmetal surfaces with a single blood-red heraldic border inside the lid. Bronze foil on the wordmark. Chain mail texture debossed into the outer lid.

The interior is different: black suede liner with a silver foil "último de su estirpe" stamped at the base. The vial sits in a precision-cut foam inset. There's a small card — matte black, letterpress — with the scent profile and your chess move notation.

♔ Outer Box

The Armor

Material: 2mm rigid chipboard, matte black laminate, chain mail texture deboss on lid.

Closure: Magnetic ribbon pull — no tab, no tuck. It opens like a vault.

Exterior detail: Bronze foil wordmark. Gunmetal chess king crest above brand name. Hairline red border inset 3mm from edge.

Outer size: Custom-fit per tier (Peón / Caballero / Último).

♟ Interior

The Chamber

Liner: Black suede paper — tactile, not glossy. Silver foil "último de su estirpe" stamp at base.

Inset: Precision-cut black foam for the vial. Zero movement. The vial is not rattling.

Insert card: Matte black, 300gsm. Letterpress in silver: scent name, house, notes, and the chess move notation of their tier. Blind emboss of the king piece.

♞ The Vial

El Frasco

Glass: Heavy-walled clear or dark amber glass depending on tier. Peón = slender 2ml; Caballero = cylindrical 5ml; Último = wide-base 10ml atomizer.

Cap: Gunmetal aluminum. Screw thread, not push-on. The weight should be noticeable.

Label: Matte black paper wrap. Foil crest + scent name in Cormorant. Tier piece (♟ ♞ ♔) on the base band.

⚔ Heraldic Motif

The Crest System

Primary mark: ♔ — Unicode king, set in Cormorant at display weight. No custom illustration needed at launch; the piece is the crest.

Secondary mark: "⚔ ♔ ⚔" — crossed swords framing the king. Used on box lid interior, insert card header, and wax seal (future).

Future: Custom illustrated heraldic crest combining a chess king with a Castilian castle tower. Commission when budget allows.
